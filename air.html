BY AIR

Vancouver Island is easily accessible by air. The island has three major airports, located in Victoria, Nanaimo, and Comox. Each airport offers a variety of services, including car rentals, taxis, and shuttles. If you're flying into Vancouver International Airport (YVR), you can take a short flight to one of the island's airports. Or, you can take a scenic ferry ride from the mainland to Vancouver Island.
