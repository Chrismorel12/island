When someone's feeling sad, it's often best to start by simply listening to them. Let them share their feelings without trying to immediately fix things. Showing empathy is key - let them know you understand their feelings are valid and you're there for them. Sometimes, just having someone to talk to can make a big difference. You can also gently encourage them to do things they enjoy or to engage in activities that might lift their spirits, like going for a walk or watching a favorite movie. Remember, it's not about having all the answers, but about being a supportive presence.
